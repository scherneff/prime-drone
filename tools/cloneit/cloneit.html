    How CloneIt works

    What does CloneIt do?

    CloneIt creates a new repoless AEM site by:

    1. Creating the AEM site config – Copies the template site configuration and points it to a new content source
    2. Creating the DA content repo – Creates a new folder in Document Authoring (DA) for your site's content

    Site naming

    Use lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only (e.g. my-site, project-2024).

    After cloning

    Your new site shares the same codebase as the template. Add content in DA and preview/publish to see it live.

    Bulk Preview / Publish

    After a successful clone, use Bulk Preview/Publish. URLs are copied to your clipboard and a modal guides you to the DA Bulk app, where you paste and run preview or publish.
